# Market Research — Quality Criteria

## Completeness
- TAM, SAM, SOM all present with numeric values and methodology
- At least 3 problems identified with severity scores
- At least 2 opportunities with scores and justification
- At least 2 trends with direction indicators
- At least 2 risks with likelihood/impact

## Structure
- All outputs in correct JSON format
- Scores are numeric, within valid ranges
- No placeholder or generic text

## Coherence
- TAM > SAM > SOM (logical hierarchy)
- Opportunities align with identified problems
- Risks relate to the actual market, not generic

## Red Flags
- Unrealistic market sizes (check order of magnitude)
- No evidence or sources cited
- All opportunities scored identically
