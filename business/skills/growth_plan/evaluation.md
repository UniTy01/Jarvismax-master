# Growth Plan — Quality Criteria

## Completeness
- Growth model identifies primary and secondary engine with rationale
- At least 3 channels ranked by ROI with effort/cost/timeline
- Retention strategy includes activation milestone, aha moment, and churn signals
- Metrics framework has north star + input + output + health metrics
- At least 5 experiments with ICE scores
- 12-week timeline with phased milestones

## Structure
- Channels include comparable data (effort, cost, timeline, volume)
- Experiments have hypothesis, metric, score, and timeline
- Timeline is phased (foundation → test → scale → review)

## Coherence
- Channels align with the growth model (content channel for content-led growth)
- Experiments target the biggest bottleneck in the funnel
- Timeline is realistic for the team size and resources
- North star metric captures real value delivery

## Red Flags
- Growth model doesn't match the product type
- All channels are paid with no organic
- No retention strategy (acquisition-only plan)
- Experiments are vague ("try SEO") without hypothesis
- Timeline assumes instant results from slow channels
