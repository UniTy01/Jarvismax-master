# Customer Persona — Reasoning Structure

## Step 1: Demographics & Context
- Name, age, role, company size, industry
- Daily responsibilities and challenges
- Technology comfort level

## Step 2: Pain Points (top 5)
- What keeps them up at night?
- Score each: frequency × severity × urgency
- Which are addressed by current solutions?

## Step 3: Motivations
- Why would they look for a solution now?
- Internal pressure (boss, metrics, growth)
- External pressure (competition, regulation, market)

## Step 4: Objections
- Price concerns and framing
- Trust and credibility barriers
- Implementation fears
- For each: the response that resolves it

## Step 5: Trigger Events
- What events make them actively search?
- Season, funding, hiring, churn spike, competitor move

## Step 6: Decision Drivers
- What makes them choose one solution over another?
- Speed, price, trust, features, integration, support
