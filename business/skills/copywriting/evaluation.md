# Copywriting — Quality Criteria

## Completeness
- 5 headlines using different persuasion angles
- Value proposition with headline, subheadline, and 3 supporting points
- 5 CTAs with different commitment levels
- At least 3 social proof elements
- 5 objection handlers with acknowledge-reframe-resolve pattern
- 5 email subject lines for different campaign types

## Structure
- Headlines under 12 words each
- CTAs use action verbs
- Objection handlers follow acknowledge → reframe → resolve
- All text is product-specific (not interchangeable with another product)

## Coherence
- Tone is consistent across all copy elements
- Headlines and value prop tell the same story
- CTAs match the awareness level
- Objection handlers address real concerns for this audience

## Red Flags
- Generic copy that could apply to any product
- All headlines use the same angle
- CTAs are just "Buy Now" variations
- Objection responses are dismissive rather than empathetic
- Subject lines are clickbait with no product relevance
