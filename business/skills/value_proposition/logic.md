# Value Proposition Design — Reasoning Structure

## Step 1: Customer Profile (right side of canvas)
- **Jobs**: What are they trying to accomplish? (functional, social, emotional)
- **Pains**: What frustrates them? What risks do they face?
- **Gains**: What outcomes do they desire? What would delight them?

## Step 2: Value Map (left side of canvas)
- **Products & services**: What you offer
- **Pain relievers**: How you eliminate/reduce specific pains
- **Gain creators**: How you create/increase specific gains

## Step 3: Fit Assessment
- Map each pain reliever to a specific pain
- Map each gain creator to a specific gain
- Score fit: how many pains/gains are addressed?
- Identify unaddressed pains (opportunity or out of scope)

## Step 4: Headline
- Formula: "We help [customer] [achieve outcome] by [mechanism]"
- Must be specific, not generic
- Under 15 words

## Step 5: Elevator Pitch
- 30 seconds, 3 sentences max
- Problem → Solution → Proof point
