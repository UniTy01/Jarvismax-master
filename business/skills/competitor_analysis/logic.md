# Competitor Analysis — Reasoning Structure

## Step 1: Competitor Identification
- Direct competitors (same product category)
- Indirect competitors (different approach, same problem)
- Emerging competitors (new entrants, funded startups)

## Step 2: Feature Matrix
- List 8-12 key features
- Score each competitor: has/partial/missing
- Identify feature parity and unique features

## Step 3: Positioning Map
- Plot competitors on price (low→high) vs quality/completeness (low→high)
- Identify clusters and white space
- Map funding and growth trajectory

## Step 4: Gap Analysis
- Features no competitor offers well
- Customer segments underserved
- Pricing gaps (too expensive or too cheap for segment)

## Step 5: Threat Assessment
- Which competitors are growing fastest?
- Which have the most funding?
- Which are most likely to enter your niche?
