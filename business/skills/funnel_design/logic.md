# Funnel Design — Reasoning Structure

## Step 1: Stage Definition (AIDA)
- **Awareness**: How do prospects discover you?
- **Interest**: What makes them engage further?
- **Decision**: What convinces them to choose you?
- **Action**: What triggers the purchase?

## Step 2: Content Strategy per Stage
- Awareness: blog posts, social media, ads
- Interest: demos, case studies, webinars
- Decision: comparisons, testimonials, free trials
- Action: pricing pages, checkout optimization, urgency

## Step 3: Metrics per Stage
- Awareness: impressions, reach, traffic
- Interest: engagement rate, time on site, email signups
- Decision: trial starts, demo requests, cart adds
- Action: conversion rate, revenue, average order value

## Step 4: Automation Touchpoints
- Email sequences (welcome, nurture, abandoned cart)
- Retargeting ads
- Chatbot engagement
- Scheduling tools

## Step 5: Bottleneck Prediction
- Where will drop-off be highest?
- What can be optimized at each transition?
- A/B test priorities
