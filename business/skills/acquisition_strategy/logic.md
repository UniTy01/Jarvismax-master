# Acquisition Strategy — Reasoning Structure

## Step 1: Channel Identification
- List all viable channels for this product/market
- Score each: reach × cost × conversion_likelihood × speed

## Step 2: Organic vs Paid Split
- Given budget and timeline, recommend split
- Organic: SEO, content, community, referrals
- Paid: ads, influencers, sponsorships
- Justify the split with expected returns

## Step 3: Content Leverage
- What content types resonate with this persona?
- Distribution channels for content
- Content-to-conversion funnel

## Step 4: Early Traction (First 30 Days)
- List 5-10 specific, actionable tactics
- Each with expected outcome and effort level
- Prioritize by speed-to-result

## Step 5: Metrics
- Define 5 KPIs to measure acquisition
- Include targets for month 1 and month 3
