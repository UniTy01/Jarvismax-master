# Pricing Strategy — Reasoning Structure

## Step 1: Willingness-to-Pay Analysis
- What does the target customer currently spend on alternatives?
- What is the perceived value of the solution?
- Price sensitivity: elastic vs inelastic demand?

## Step 2: Model Selection
- Subscription (monthly/annual), usage-based, one-time, freemium, per-seat
- Match model to customer buying behavior
- Justify choice with market evidence

## Step 3: Tier Design
- 2-4 tiers maximum
- Clear feature differentiation between tiers
- Anchor pricing: middle tier should be the target

## Step 4: Unit Economics
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- Lifetime Value (LTV) — LTV/CAC ratio target: >3
- Gross margin per tier
- Break-even timeline

## Step 5: Competitive Positioning
- Map price vs feature set against top 3 competitors
- Identify pricing gap (underserved segment)
- Decide: premium, market-rate, or penetration pricing
