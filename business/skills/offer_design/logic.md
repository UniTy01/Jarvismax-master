# Offer Design — Reasoning Structure

## Step 1: Problem-Solution Fit
- Restate the problem in customer language
- Define the exact solution boundary
- Identify what is NOT included

## Step 2: Value Proposition
- Who is this for? (specific persona)
- What do they get? (concrete outcomes)
- Why choose this over alternatives?
- What is the measurable ROI?

## Step 3: Offer Structure
- Define 1-3 tiers (e.g., Starter/Pro/Enterprise)
- List features per tier with clear boundaries
- Define delivery model (SaaS, service, hybrid)

## Step 4: Pricing
- Choose model: subscription, usage, project, hybrid
- Set price points with justification
- Compare to alternatives and willingness-to-pay

## Step 5: Differentiation
- List 3-5 concrete differentiators
- For each: what competitors do vs what you do differently
- Identify defensible advantages

## Step 6: USP
- One sentence that captures the core selling point
- Must be specific, not generic
